GRAPHIC DESIGN

UI/UX: Designing and Decoding the Web Experience #SLSDesign

What actually makes up a social media platform, the look, feel, buttons, colors, logos, layout - whether it's online or on mobile - greatly affects how we use them and their individual success. User Interface and User Experience (UI/UX) has become ever more relevant in the design world as people continue to increase their social media consumption, especially on mobile devices. Factors such as language, location, experience level and more go into the research of UI/UX designers who must satisfy the needs of millions of users with the same set of consistent and simple features. This panel will discuss what goes into the design of modern web and mobile social apps as well as the principles of UI/UX for designers and non-designers alike.

The rapid evolution of social media has been hugely influential in the world of sports. Social media platforms enable fans and enthusiasts to connect directly to their favorite teams, comment on games and developments as they happen, and connect with other fans. Social media use during major sporting events like the Sochi Olympics and March Madness has transformed the future of sports coverage and analysis. This panel will explore how sports and social media come together and what this crossover means for fans, athletes, teams, and professionals in the field.

STARTUPS

The First Follower: Social Media & Startups #SLSstartup

To startup small and make it to the top, you have to have a social media strategy as big as your dreams. Startup companies face many challenges as they grow, but quite often these meager beginnings offer creative minds the opportunity to create outstanding examples of social media engagement. Innovation starts somewhere and social media is swiftly becoming the secret to propelling that idea forward. From metrics and measurement to creative application, we'll talk to successful startups and entrepreneurs about how they have navigated the intersection of online promotion and real-world business.
